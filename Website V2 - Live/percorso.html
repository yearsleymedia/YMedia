The Service

Every session planned.

We build the structure that keeps your training consistent, progressive, and on track — so you can focus on the training itself.

Fully Custom Plans
Running, gym, or both — built from your goal, your current times, and how you actually train. No templates, no one-size-fits-all.
Your Own Interactive Tracker

Why Percorso

Built for those who want structured training,
without the guesswork.

01
No more guessing what today is
Walking into the gym without a plan, or heading out for a run with no idea what pace — it wastes sessions and stalls progress. With Percorso, you open your tracker and today is already decided: the session, the targets, the structure. All you do is train.
02
A plan that fits your actual life
Generic plans put the long run on Sunday and assume a 9-to-5. Real life has night shifts, rotations, and weeks that don't cooperate. Percorso is built around your schedule from day one — so the plan works with your life instead of fighting it.
03
Structure that moves with you
A PDF is the same on week twelve as it was on day one. Your Percorso tracker isn't — log a new best time and every pace recalculates, every target tightens. As you get fitter, faster, and stronger, the plan rises to meet you.
