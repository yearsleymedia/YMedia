Clause 01
The Service

Percorso by Y Media agrees to create written social media content for the client based on the package selected. The scope of work is as follows:

The Percorso Plan — £60One fully custom training plan — running, gym, or hybrid — delivered as a private interactive tracker. The tracker includes the full session-by-session plan, personal pace zones calculated from the client's submitted times (where applicable), automatic recalculation of paces as new times are logged, session tick-off and progress logging, and scheduling built around the client's stated availability and shift pattern. Clients who already have a plan of their own may instead have that plan digitised into the tracker as written. Delivered as a private link within 3 working days of payment being received and the onboarding form being completed.

All content is delivered as a branded Percorso document sent by email. Delivery timelines begin once the onboarding form has been fully completed and received — not from the date of payment.

Clause 03
Refund Policy

Due to the bespoke nature of this service, refunds are not available once the plan has been built and delivered.

If a client believes the plan does not reflect what was agreed, they should contact Percorso by Y Media within 3 days of delivery. We will review the onboarding responses and, where reasonable, make amendments at no extra charge.

One round of amendments is included as standard. This covers adjustments to scheduling, session structure, or training paces — not a complete rebuild of the plan.

What Is and Isn't Included

Percorso provides training structure and planning only. Percorso does not provide medical advice, physiotherapy, injury treatment, live coaching, form checks, or personal training sessions, and does not guarantee any specific results including race times, weight loss, or strength gains. The client confirms the information provided in their onboarding form — including any injuries or health limitations — is accurate, and that they are fit to undertake physical training. Clients with a health condition should consult a medical professional before starting any training plan.
