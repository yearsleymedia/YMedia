Email 03  You send
Plan Delivery
Send when the training plan is ready — include the portal link
SubjectPercorso — Your Plan is Ready
Hi [Name], Your training plan is ready. You can view it at the link below — it's been set up on your personal Percorso portal and is accessible any time: 👉 [Client portal URL] Your plan has been built around everything you told us — your goals, your schedule, and your current level. Read through it before your first session so you know what to expect. A couple of things worth knowing: — You have one round of amendments included. If anything doesn't feel right once you've reviewed it, just reply within 3 days and let us know what you'd like adjusted. — We'd also recommend adding the portal to your phone's home screen so it's easy to access on the go. Instructions for both iPhone and Android are included at the bottom of this email. It's been great putting this together for you — enjoy the process. — Percorso by Y Media ────────────────────────────── HOW TO ADD YOUR PLAN TO YOUR PHONE ────────────────────────────── Save your Percorso portal to your home screen so it opens like an app — no login, one tap. ── iPhone (Safari) ── 1. Open the portal link in Safari (not Chrome). 2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up. 3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen". 4. Give it a name (e.g. My Plan) and tap Add. 5. It will now appear on your home screen like any other app. ── Android (Chrome) ── 1. Open the portal link in Chrome. 2. Tap the three dots in the top-right corner. 3. Tap "Add to Home screen". 4. Give it a name and tap Add (or hold and drag it to your home screen if prompted). 5. It will now appear on your home screen and open directly to your plan. If you run into any issues getting it set up, just reply here and I'll help you through it.
