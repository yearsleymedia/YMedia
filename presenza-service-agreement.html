Clause 01
The Service

Presenza by Y Media agrees to create written social media content for the client based on the package selected. The scope of work is as follows:

Tier 1 — £3015 ready-to-post written captions. Each caption is structured with a Theme (the topic), a Hook (the opening line), and the full Caption text. The client supplies their own photos or videos — Presenza provides the words only. Delivered within 5 working days of the completed onboarding form being received. Tier 2 — £4030 pieces of written content: 20 captions, 5 reel scripts, and 5 carousel sets. Each piece is structured with a Theme, Hook, and full written text. Clients who do not post video content will have reel scripts replaced with additional captions. The client supplies their own photos or videos — Presenza provides the words only. Delivered within 7 working days of the completed onboarding form being received. Tier 3 Premium — £8030 pieces of written content as above, plus custom branded visual designs for every caption post and every carousel slide. Reel scripts are written content only — no visual is produced for these. Clients who do not post video content will have reel scripts replaced with additional captions. Delivered within 10 working days of the completed onboarding form being received.

All content is delivered as a branded Presenza document sent by email. Delivery timelines begin once the onboarding form has been fully completed and received — not from the date of payment.

Clause 02
Payment

Payment is required in full before any work begins. The onboarding form will only be sent once payment has been confirmed. By agreeing to these terms, the client confirms they have read and understood everything set out in this document.

Clause 03
Refund Policy

Due to the bespoke nature of this service, refunds are not available once the content has been written and delivered.

If a client believes the content does not reflect what was agreed, they should contact Presenza by Y Media within 3 days of delivery. We will review the onboarding responses and, where reasonable, make amendments at no extra charge.

One round of amendments is included as standard. This covers adjustments to tone, wording, or topic — not a complete rewrite of all content.
Clause 04
What Is and Isn't Included

Presenza provides written content under Tier 1 and Tier 2. Custom branded visuals are included in Tier 3 only. Presenza does not post content on the client's behalf, manage social media accounts, or guarantee any specific results including follower growth, engagement rates, or leads.

Clause 05
Ownership

Once delivered and paid for, all content belongs to the client. Y Media retains the right to reference the work for portfolio or promotional purposes unless the client requests otherwise in writing.

Clause 06
Confidentiality

Any information shared by the client during onboarding is used solely to create their content. Y Media will not share, sell, or disclose client information to any third party.
